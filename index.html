About Us

Welcome to Desa Sumurlaban

Desa Sumurlaban is a picturesque village known for its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty. Our community is dedicated to preserving our traditions while embracing modernity.

Our History

Established in the early 19th century, Desa Sumurlaban has a rich history of cultural and agricultural development. Our village has evolved over the years, yet it still maintains its traditional charm and values.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to preserve and promote our cultural heritage while fostering sustainable development. Our vision is to become a model village known for its harmony, hospitality, and vibrant community life.

Community Services

We offer various community services including health care, education, and local businesses support. Our village is committed to improving the quality of life for all residents through collaborative efforts and innovation.
